Cat Activities

Enrichment Ideas

Keeping your cat mentally stimulated and physically active is essential for their overall well-being, and enrichment activities are a great way to achieve this. From interactive toys and puzzle feeders to climbing trees, scratching posts, and window perches, there are many ways to engage your cat’s natural instincts.

Cat Food

Choosing the Right Cat Food

Choosing between wet and dry cat food can play an important role in supporting your cat’s overall health. Many cat owners find that a balanced mix of both works well, depending on their cat’s age, preferences, and dietary needs.

How Much to Feed

Knowing how much to feed your cat is key to maintaining a healthy weight. The right portion size depends on factors such as your cat’s age, weight, activity level, and whether they are eating wet food, dry food, or a combination of both.
